Digital Agriculture Club of Purdue

We are a club focused on the role of digital technologies in food and agriculture systems.
Join us if you'd like to chat about tech topics (open source, generative AI, trust & privacy), learn new skills (programming, modeling, how to use IoT), network with peers + professionals in this space, and have fun!
Anyone of any major or discipline welcome! Grads and undergrads!

We meet on Thursdays in ABE 3110 5.30-6.30pm.
Join our slack to stay up to date!
