What is it?

Our club was one of four recipients of the Boiler Gold Rush Entertainment Challenge grant to create an interactive installation for Fall 2024 orientation.

It will be a showdown of robots in a 16'x16' arena, where players aim to collect carrots as a remote-controlled rabbit without getting caught by our autonomous Scarecrow Bot.
