What is it?

Animals like deer and rabbits can be cute, but not when they eat your horticultural crops!

Our solution is an automated, humane vertebrate pest management that uses a variety of stimulus to keep them on their toes.

We are currently building a prototype using TurtleBot but the goal for field applications is The Amiga. We hope to test this at the Purdue Student Farms.

Components being used

  • Computer vision
  • Autonomous navigation
  • Electrical engineering
  • User experience
