--- layout: project title: Scarecrow Bot permalink: /scarecrow-bot/ long-description: Animals like deer and rabbits can be cute, but not when they eat your horticultural crops!

Our solution is an automated, humane vertebrate pest management that uses a variety of stimulus to keep them on their toes. ---
We are building a prototype using the TurtleBot but our goal for field applications is with The Amiga. We hope to test this at the Purdue Student Farm.
